# Water Quality AI Map

An AI-powered environmental monitoring application that integrates Google Earth Engine, water-quality measurements, geospatial mapping, and Large Language Models (LLMs) for water quality assessment and interpretation.

## Features

* Interactive map-based location selection
* Google Earth Engine climate and environmental data extraction
* Water quality parameter analysis:

  * pH
  * TDS (Total Dissolved Solids)
  * TH (Total Hardness)
  * Calcium (Ca)
  * Magnesium (Mg)
  * Dissolved Oxygen (DO)
  * Biological Oxygen Demand (BOD)
* Water quality risk classification:

  * Very Low
  * Low
  * Moderate
  * High
  * Very High
* AI-generated environmental interpretation and recommendations
* Integration of historical water quality datasets
* Interactive visualization of risk intensity on maps
* Streamlit Cloud deployment support

## Technology Stack

* Python
* Streamlit
* Google Earth Engine
* Folium
* Pandas
* Scikit-learn
* Hugging Face Transformers
* Geospatial Analytics

## Applications

* Water quality monitoring
* Environmental risk assessment
* Surface and groundwater studies
* Smart environmental decision support
* Research and education
